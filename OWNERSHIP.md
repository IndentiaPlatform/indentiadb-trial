# Ownership

## Ownership and licence status

Repository status: **Trial / evaluation**

This repository uses repo-level notices as evidence and operating policy, not as
the legal transfer mechanism.

- For employees contributing in the course of employment, Indentia B.V. relies on the Dutch statutory employer rule and matching employment terms.
- For contractors and freelancers, contributions require a signed written assignment deed in favour of Indentia B.V.
- For upstream or third-party code, the original licence terms stay in force.

Use is limited to trial, evaluation, or other written commercial terms. Public or unsolicited external contributions are not accepted.

See the root notice file in this repository and the internal repository-rights
pack under `idea/docs/compliance/repository-rights/`.
